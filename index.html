About Us

At Lontra, we are pioneering the development of advanced biomaterials to transform healthcare and technology. Our mission is to harness nature-inspired design and cutting-edge science to create sustainable, high-performance solutions for the future. At Lontra Bio, we develop synthetic polypeptides that capture the structure and function of native proteins while leveraging the versatility and precision of chemically produced polymers. Our bio-inspired materials are designed to address challenges in therapeutics, biomaterials, and biological preservation, providing innovative solutions for mucin and glycocalyx models, anti-infectives, and cryopreservation.
